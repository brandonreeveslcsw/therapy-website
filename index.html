About Brandon

I'm a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with over 25 years of experience working with adults and young adults. My work is thoughtful, practical, and depth-oriented — focused on understanding patterns that shape how you think, feel, and relate, while staying grounded in real-world change.

I use a variety of approaches, including EMDR, and tailor therapy to the individual rather than relying on a one-size-fits-all model.
