Consultation & Training

Professional consultation and continuing education for clinicians in Louisiana.

Clinical Consultation

I offer consultation for licensed clinicians seeking perspective on clinical challenges, ethical questions, practice transitions, or professional direction.

Whether you're navigating a complex case, considering a shift in your practice, or working through an ethical dilemma, consultation provides a structured space to think things through with an experienced colleague.

  • Complex case consultation
  • Ethical decision-making
  • Practice development and transitions
  • Professional direction and career planning
  • Clinical program development

Training & Continuing Education

I am available for training engagements and continuing education presentations for organizations, agencies, and professional groups. Topics can be tailored to the needs of your group and may include clinical practice, ethics, supervision, and professional development.

If you're interested in scheduling a training or CE event, please reach out to discuss your needs.

Disclosure: Brandon Reeves currently serves on the Louisiana State Board of Social Work Examiners. Consultation and training services are offered in his private capacity and are independent of his role on the Board. Any matters related to licensure, complaints, or Board proceedings are handled exclusively through the Board's official processes.
