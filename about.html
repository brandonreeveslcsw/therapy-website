About Brandon Reeves

Licensed Clinical Social Worker · Baton Rouge, Louisiana

My Approach

Therapy works best when it is both serious and human.

People come to therapy because something isn't working the way it should. Anxiety, depression, past experiences, or life transitions can leave you feeling stuck or off balance. Sometimes there's no single problem — just a sense that something needs to change.

My role is to help you slow things down, gain clarity, and move forward with steadiness and purpose. We will work together to identify what is causing you discomfort in your life and find ways forward that feel manageable and sustainable.

Therapy isn't something that someone does to you. It's something the therapist does with you.

Experience & Credentials

I'm a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with over 25 years of experience working with adults and young adults. My work is thoughtful, practical, and depth-oriented — focused on understanding patterns that shape how you think, feel, and relate, while staying grounded in real-world change.

I use a variety of therapeutic approaches, including EMDR, and tailor therapy to the individual rather than relying on a one-size-fits-all model.

  • Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) — 25+ years of practice
  • Board-Approved Clinical Supervisor (BACS)
  • Member, Louisiana State Board of Social Work Examiners
  • EMDR trained
  • I see clients at Baton Rouge Counseling Associates, located at 10935 Perkins Rd, Baton Rouge, LA

Areas of Focus

  • Anxiety and depression
  • Trauma and unresolved past experiences
  • Life transitions and uncertainty
  • Men's issues — stress, identity, direction
  • Young adult development — college-age and early career
  • Burnout and motivation
  • Relationship patterns

How I Work

My approach is collaborative, paced, and focused on meaningful change — without pressure to move faster than feels right. I meet clients where they are, whether that means processing something specific or simply making sense of a general feeling that something is off.

Sessions are available in-person at my Baton Rouge office or via secure telehealth for anyone located in Louisiana.
