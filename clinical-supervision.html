Clinical Supervision

Board-Approved Clinical Supervision for social workers in Louisiana.

I provide clinical supervision to social workers at various stages of licensure who are looking to deepen clinical skill, strengthen judgment, and develop confidence in their work.

Supervision is collaborative, structured, and reflective — designed to support your professional development while meeting the requirements of the Louisiana State Board of Social Work Examiners.

My Approach to Supervision

Good supervision should challenge you to think more deeply about your clinical work while providing a safe, supportive space to do so. I bring the same thoughtfulness and depth to supervision that I bring to therapy — with a focus on developing your own clinical voice and judgment rather than simply providing answers.

  • Case conceptualization and treatment planning
  • Clinical skill development
  • Professional identity and confidence
  • Ethical decision-making
  • Navigating complex clinical situations

Credentials

  • Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) — 25+ years
  • Board-Approved Clinical Supervisor (BACS)
  • Member, Louisiana State Board of Social Work Examiners

Getting Started

If you're a social worker seeking clinical supervision in Louisiana, I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss whether we'd be a good fit. Reach out to schedule an initial conversation.

Disclosure: Brandon Reeves currently serves on the Louisiana State Board of Social Work Examiners. Supervision services are offered in his private capacity and are independent of his role on the Board. Supervisees should be aware of this dual role. Any matters related to licensure, complaints, or Board proceedings are handled exclusively through the Board's official processes.
